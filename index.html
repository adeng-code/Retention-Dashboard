Per-Institution Model Tuning

The same student profile can be high-risk at one school and on-track at another. RetainIQ learns from each institution's own historical outcomes — so the signals that matter at your school are the ones that drive your model.

Highbar University

Grade-compressed elite

At Highbar, grade inflation means a 3.0 GPA is not a distress signal — nearly everyone earns above a 3.0. The model learns this and down-weights GPA accordingly. Instead, LMS disengagement and gateway course failure are the dominant predictors here.

Open / shopping curriculum

Openwood encourages students to drop classes and explore. Course-dropping is a feature of the academic culture, not a distress signal. The model suppresses drops entirely — otherwise it might falsely flag exploratory students or even treat drops as protective.

Large public institution
